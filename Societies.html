GDSC

Welcome to Google Developer Student Club at Chitkara University. Developer Student Clubs (DSC) are community groups for students from any academic background in their undergraduate or graduate term. By joining a DSC, students build their professional and personal network, get access to Google Developer Resources, and work together to build solutions for local problems in a peer - to - peer learning environment.Click HereTo Know More.

MATRIX

The Matrix is a club for future technocrats; for people who have the passion and zeal to excel in co-curricular activities, besides achieving academic excellence. The Department of Computer Applications had a long-standing wish to find a platform for its students where they could rediscover themselves and nurture their skills. It was the collective efforts of the students of the MCA and BCA Programs—along with support from the faculty—that led to the formation of the first-ever club of the Department on 15 February, 2007.Click HereTO know More.
