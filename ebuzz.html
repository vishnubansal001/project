Abobut E-Buzz

E-Buzz, a society of electronics engineers, was formed in 2003 and since then it has nurtured talent and that too conscientiously.

E-Buzz is all about crafting professional technocrats and for this, the society has made immense efforts to systematize and organize various activities and events that help us in realizing our aim of fostering budding talent.

We realize that acquiring a degree alone will not suffice for a professional, a personality that reflects efforts taken, experiences learnt and attempts made to acquire more during the didactic period, will emerge as a winner in the end. We intend to inculcate these aspects in order to create absolute technocrats.

Ever since its inauguration, continuous innovation and rapid transformation have been its underlying themes. E-Buzz relates with the French phrase La elettronica erudite—the name given to a castle made with semiconductor chips by a renowned scientist.
